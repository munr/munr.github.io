Day Countdown Chrome Extension Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8th 2024

Thank you for using Day Countdown Chrome Extension ("the Extension"). This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and explains how we handle information collected during your use of the Extension.

Information Collection:

We want to reassure our users that Day Countdown does not collect any personal data. We do not store, process, or transmit any information that can be used to personally identify you. The Extension operates as a standalone tool with a focus on simplicity and functionality.

Usage Data:

Day Countdown may collect non-personal, aggregate data for the purpose of improving user experience and Extension functionality. This data is general and does not include any personally identifiable information. Usage data may include the number of users, frequency of use, and common features accessed.

Changes to the Privacy Policy:

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and it is your responsibility to review the Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions or concerns about the privacy practices of Day Countdown, please contact us at day-countdown-extension@munr.dev.

By using the Day Countdown Chrome Extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. It is essential to review this policy regularly for any updates.
